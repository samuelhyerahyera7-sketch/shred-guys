Our Services

Certified, compliant paper shredding solutions for businesses of all sizes across Gauteng.

On-Site Shredding

Our mobile shredding truck comes directly to your location. Watch your documents destroyed on the spot in our industrial on-board shredder — ideal for businesses that need witnessed, high-security destruction. Nothing leaves your premises unshredded.

Get a Quote →

Off-Site Shredding

We collect your documents in our confidential wheelie bins, seal them, and transport them to our facility A Certificate of Destruction is issued after every job. More affordable than on-site.

Get a Quote →

Recurring Shredding

Set up a regular shredding schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. We provide locking consoles or wheelie bins for your office and empty them on every visit. The most cost-effective way to maintain ongoing POPIA compliance.

Get a Quote →

One-Time Purge / Office Cleanup

Moving offices? Year-end cleanout? We supply 240L wheelie bins, leave them with you for up to two weeks for filling, then collect and shred everything. Handles any volume — from a few bags to hundreds of boxes.

Get a Quote →

Client Delivers / Walk-In

Bring your documents directly to our facility. We help you offload your vehicle, weigh your load on our scale, and you are welcome to witness the shredding process. Charged per kilogram — our most affordable option.

Get a Quote →

Bin & Console Rental

Choose from 120L and 240L lockable plastic wheelie bins, Leather Touch ABS office bins (Maxi & Medi), and Junior or Standard melamine wood consoles. Monthly rental available with or without a service contract.
